Welcome to Our Shelter Gallery!

At Shelter Foot Gallery, we believe every cat deserves a second chance at happiness. We rescue cats of all breeds and backgrounds—giving them a safe, loving environment while they wait for their forever homes. Every cat in our care receives medical treatment, socialization, and individual attention to help them thrive. Whether you're looking to adopt a playful kitten or a calm senior companion, our goal is to match you with the perfect furry friend. Join us in making a life-changing difference—one paw at a time.
